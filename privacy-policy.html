Privacy Policy

Trivia Plus

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trivia Plus ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By downloading or using Trivia Plus, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We and our third-party partners collect the following types of information:

Information stored on your device

Trivia Plus saves your game progress, wallet balance (coins and XP), profile settings, preferences, and achievement data locally on your device using iOS system storage (UserDefaults). This data does not leave your device unless a third-party SDK (described below) transmits it as part of its normal operation.

Usage and analytics data (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics, provided by Google LLC, to understand how players interact with the App. Firebase Analytics may automatically collect:

  • App events (e.g., screens visited, game rounds completed)
  • Session duration and engagement metrics
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • General geographic region (country or city level)
  • An anonymous device identifier

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We use it only to improve the App experience. Firebase Analytics does not collect your name, email address, or precise location.

Crash and diagnostics data (Firebase Crashlytics)

We use Firebase Crashlytics to detect and diagnose app crashes and errors. Crashlytics may collect:

  • Stack traces and crash logs
  • Device state at the time of a crash
  • A randomly generated installation UUID (not linked to your identity)

This data is used solely to fix bugs and improve stability.

Advertising data (Google AdMob)

Trivia Plus displays ads served by Google AdMob, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect and use:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt
  • IP address (used to serve region-appropriate ads)
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

If you decline the ATT permission prompt, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads and will not use your IDFA for tracking. You can change your choice at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Game Center data

If you are signed into Apple Game Center, your Game Center alias and scores may be submitted to leaderboards. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and is not stored by us independently.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the App and its features
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues
  • To understand general usage patterns and player engagement
  • To display advertisements that help keep the App free
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Trivia Plus integrates the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Game Center — apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Trivia API — used to fetch quiz questions; no personally identifiable information is sent in these requests beyond a standard IP address.

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, we will ask for your permission before allowing AdMob to access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for cross-app tracking purposes. You may decline this request without affecting your ability to use the App. Non-personalized ads will be shown instead.

5. Data Retention

Game data stored locally on your device is retained until you delete the App or clear its data. Analytics and crash data retained by Google Firebase is subject to Google's data retention policies, typically 14 months for Analytics data. AdMob data retention is governed by Google's policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Trivia Plus is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Decline the ATT prompt, or go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable tracking for Trivia Plus.
  • Reset your advertising identifier: Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → and use "Reset Advertising Identifier."
  • Delete your local data: Delete the App from your device to remove all locally stored game data.
  • Opt out of Firebase Analytics: Contact us at the email below and we can disable analytics collection for your installation.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Game data is stored locally on your device and is protected by iOS's built-in security. However, no method of data storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
