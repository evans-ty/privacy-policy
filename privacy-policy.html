Overview

Trivia Plus ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to that information when you use our mobile application.

By using Trivia Plus, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to operate and improve the app:

  • Usage data — game scores, session activity, and feature interactions, collected anonymously.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and app version, used for crash reporting and compatibility.
  • Purchase information — when you make an in-app purchase, Apple processes the transaction. We receive confirmation that a purchase occurred but do not receive your payment details.
  • Crash and diagnostic data — automatically collected to help us identify and fix technical issues.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

In-App Purchases

Trivia Plus offers in-app purchases processed exclusively through Apple's App Store. All billing, payment, and subscription management is handled by Apple. We do not store or process any payment card information.

To manage or cancel a subscription, visit your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Refund requests are handled by Apple in accordance with their refund policy.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Operate and maintain app functionality
  • Diagnose crashes and technical errors
  • Improve gameplay and user experience
  • Verify and restore in-app purchases

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Trivia Plus may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store — for payment processing and app distribution
  • Apple Analytics / TestFlight — for crash reports and diagnostics (if opted in on your device)

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services relevant to you.

Data Retention

We retain anonymized usage and diagnostic data only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Data tied to a support request (e.g., an email you send us) is retained for up to 12 months after resolution.

Children's Privacy

Trivia Plus is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal information we hold. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
