Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Trivia Plus ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Ty Evans. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Trivia Plus mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name or email address. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers and advertising identifiers
  • App usage data (events, session length, in-app actions)
  • General device information (OS version, device type)
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how players interact with the App
  • Serve advertisements (which may be personalized based on your interests)
  • Track in-app purchases and restore them when needed

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics — used to collect anonymized usage analytics to help us improve the App. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple Game Center — used for leaderboards and player authentication. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — used to process in-app purchases. All transactions are handled by Apple.

4. Advertising

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests using your advertising identifier (IDFA). You can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier at any time in your iPhone Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's privacy policy for details on how purchase data is handled.

6. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores game progress, preferences, and profile data locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and local files). This data does not leave your device except as described in Section 3.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

8. Your Choices

  • Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising in your iPhone Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings.
  • Analytics: Usage analytics are collected anonymously and cannot be tied back to you personally.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
